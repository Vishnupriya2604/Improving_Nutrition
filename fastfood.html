Top 5 Fast Food Chain in U.S.

  • America loves fast food and a new study by NIH reveals just how often it’s consumed by them. About 85 million Americans eat fast food on any given day.

  • The top five fast food chains in USA based on their revenue is Mc Donald’s, Starbucks, Dunkin Donuts, Taco Bell and Burger King.

  • To understand the nutritional value of fast food I considered Mc Donald’s and Starbucks data.
