Key Findings and Conslusions

  • Focusing on Nutrition, helped me understand how it was important to a person’s well-being and what are the most important nutrients required to be healthy. According to the research and data analysis the most important nutrients required are vitamins, minerals and micronutrients and what are the specific ones required in each category.
  • From the research conducted it can be found that in united states, the most popular diets are the Ketogenic diet, Low carb and Vegan diet. An in-depth analysis of each diet shed light into the advantages and disadvantages of each. Diets do not provide the daily required nutrition a body need.
  • In this fast-paced era, everyone is looking to grab a quick bite to eat especially from fast food chains. The analysis and research helped in finding the healthier options in this fast food menu so we can make smarter choices if we have no choice than to consume fast foods.
  • Machine learning can be used to make smarter food choice, hence building a healthy food recommendation system can suggest healthier alternatives to unhealthy cravings. The model would make the effort to scan through different food options to recommend healthy food, so you don’t have to put in the effort and time in researching nutrition in food items.
  • Overall this project helped in improving nutrition using different data science tools, methods and techniques.
